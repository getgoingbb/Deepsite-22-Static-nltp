Activities

Try these simple drills. Always choose a clear, safe area and progress gradually.

Bean Bag Toss Balance Line Coordination Ladders

Bean Bag Toss

Alternate hands, vary distance, add a call-out (left/right/color) for cognitive challenge.

Balance Line

Walk a line slowly, heel-to-toe, switch directions; add head turns and object pass.

Coordination Ladders

In/out patterns, cross-overs, and rhythm changes improve footwork and timing.

Playful, step-by-step movement for better balance, coordination, and confidence at any age.
