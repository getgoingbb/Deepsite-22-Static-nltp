Meet Stephen Jepson

The visionary behind Never Leave The Playground and master of countless extraordinary skills

The Man Who Never Left The Playground

Stephen Jepson is a true renaissance man - an inventor, artist, athlete, and master of countless skills. For over 80 years, he has dedicated his life to the pursuit of play, creativity, and continuous learning.

His philosophy is simple: never stop playing, never stop learning, and never leave the playground. This mindset has allowed him to master everything from unicycling and knife throwing to juggling and rope walking.

Through his instructional videos, Stephen shares his lifetime of knowledge and experience, inspiring others to embrace play and master new skills at any age.
