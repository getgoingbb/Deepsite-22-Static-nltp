Meet Stephen Jepson

The Man Who Never Left The Playground

Stephen Jepson is an inventor, artist, athlete, and teacher. For over 80 years he has championed the power of play, creativity, and continuous learning.

His philosophy is simple: never stop playing, never stop learning. From unicycling and juggling to rope walking, Stephen shares scalable, safe progressions for any age.
