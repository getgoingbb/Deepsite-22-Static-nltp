Transportation Guides

Local Shuttles

Free shuttles run every 30 minutes from the main entrance to key attractions. Schedule: 9 AM - 6 PM.

  • Stops at Giant Slide, Maze Garden, and Zip Line
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • Real-time tracking via app

Bike Rentals

Rent bikes for $10/hour to explore trails and paths. Helmets and locks included.

  • Electric bikes available for $15/hour
  • Family bike packages
  • Pickup/drop-off at accommodations
